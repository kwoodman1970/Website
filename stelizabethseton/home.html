St. Elizabeth Seton School

Welcome to St. Elizabeth Seton School!

Our teachers and staff look forward to working with you throughout the school year. Plans have been made to help us build a school that has a strong instructional focus and a caring, Catholic school culture. We are blessed with an exceptional staff, talented students and supportive parents.

Catholic schools are here to support parents' values, and beliefs and to allow children to live out those values in an environment based on gospel values and the spiritual journey of which we are all a part. We ask your assistance, cooperation and support so that together, with St. John the Evangelist Parish, we may meet the mandate of educating your children in the Catholic academic tradition. With the guidance of the Holy Spirit, trust and mutual support we can meet any challenges and continue to develop a tradition of caring and excellence at St. Elizabeth Seton School.

This handbook is designed to familiarise you with our school. In addition, eboard, verbal and written communiqués will attempt to keep you currently informed. We then invite you to contact your child's homeroom and subject teachers first, then any of the school administrators for further assistance regarding your child's progress or information about the school. Please read this booklet and use it as a guide for future reference.

Sincerely,
