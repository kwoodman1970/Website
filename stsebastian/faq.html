St. Sebastian Elementary School

Frequently Asked Questions

  • If I live outside of Chaparral can my children attend St. Sebastian Elementary School?

    Not at this time. Only Catholic students residing in the community of Chaparral can attend St. Sebastian Elementary School.

  • What are the bell times for the school?

    Bell times for the instructional day have not yet been determined. Bell times are linked directly to bus schedules and these are still being developed across our school district. The number of students riding the bus and the availability of qualified bus drivers are important factors determining bus routes. It is expected that bus routes will be determined in this spring, and with that information bell times will be developed. Please refer to this or the District's web site for current information.

  • Will St. Sebastian Elementary School open in September?

    St. Sebastian Elementary School is on pace to open in September 2007. Parents will be notified if any changes in construction patterns occur, which could disrupt the construction schedule.

  • Our house is under construction in Chaparral. Can my child attend St. Sebastian starting in September, 2007?

    Yes. If you will be assuming possession of your home during the 2007 - 2008 school year, then your child can attend St. Sebastian Elementary School starting in September. Please be aware, however, that you would be responsible for transporting your child from your current location.

  • What grades will be offered at St. Sebastian Elementary School?

    St. Sebastian Elementary School will be offering Kindergarten through Grade 6 programming.

  • Will my child be eligible for bus transportation to and from school?

    Bus transportation is provided to Chaparral students who live within 1.25 kilometers from St. Sebastian Elementary School. Along with bus route information, the designated eligibility area for bus transportation will also be provided this spring. Maps will be drawn up and provided to parents so that eligibility is clear. Please be reminded that transportation fees are: $165/student or $330/family.
