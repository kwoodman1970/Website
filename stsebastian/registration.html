St. Sebastian Elementary School

Registration

If you are a Catholic resident of Chaparral and your child is already registered within a Calgay Catholic Shool, you are not required to register your child for St. Sebastian. Your child's name will automatically be submitted for registration.

Registration for all new students is now being facilitated at St. Albert the Great School at 13825 Parkside Dr. SE (271-5522). Please bring the following items with you when you register:

  • Child's birth certificate
  • Baptismal certificate of one parent and/or the child (if child has been baptized)
  • Documentation with current address

To be eligible for kindergarten during the 2007-2008 school year, children must be five years old by February 29, 2008.
